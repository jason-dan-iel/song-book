1
Sowing in the morning sowing seeds of kindness Sowing in the noontide and the dewy eves Waiting for the harvest and the time of reaping We shall come rejoicing bringing in the sheaves.
Chorus
Bringing in the sheaves - 2 We shall come rejoicing Bringing in the sheaves.
2
Sowing in the sunshine, sowing in the shadows Fearing neither clouds nor winter's chilling breeze By and by the harvest and the labour ended We shall come rejoicing, bringing in the sheaves.
3
Go then ever weeping sowing for the Master Tho' the loss sustained our spirit often grieves When our weeping's over, He will bid us welcome We shall come rejoicing, bringing in the sheaves.
